Few words about Lubin

Lubin, the place where we will host you, was written about in the 12th century. The Lusatian culture played a significant role even earlier. But if you really want to travel back to the times that made the biggest impact on this place, it's time to put on thick jackets and woolen socks: the next stop in the Ice Age. It was then that the Wolin island we know today was born: full of lakes, hills, wild beaches and the highest cliffs in Poland.
